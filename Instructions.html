What's it For?

To help students learn grammar given randomized subject-verb-object combinations.

How to Use

  1. Double-click index.html. This will open LinguaSlots in your browser.
  2. Copy/Paste/Replace the word lists with your own.
  3. Click the slot machine "lever" that says "Pull."
  4. A random word from each list will be selected, forming a new phrase.

That's it!
